Introduction: 17 Stages

As one navigates the infinite pathways of life, you experience a multitude of unique beings, events and emotions. Each direction chosen (whether left or right), and each act of love – all emit different colors.

前言: 17個階段

在這時間不停轉動的過程中，接觸各種獨特的人事物，向左或向右選擇，又或著為愛行事，心情的轉換，這些種種過程當下，都散發著不同的色彩。
